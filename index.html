The Process

Order

Customize your produce box with up to three changes to our offering to suit your unique taste profile.

Deliver

We'll deliver fresh island produce to your doorstep in insulated boxes anywhere in Nassau, Bahamas.

Enjoy

Your box will include recipies tailored to the produce inside, so there's no reason to waste!
